Pizza

Description

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Activate yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle.
  5. Add tomato sauce, Mozzarella, Pepperoni, bake for 400 degrees for 15 minutes
